TITLE: Bootstrapping a Computer-Based Testing Facility Using Shared Lab Space

-------------------------  METAREVIEW  ------------------------
This paper describes deploying and managing a computing lab testing facility to assess students under exam conditions but with significant flexibility. This work presents feasible and helpful tips on deploying and scaling up such a lab-based assessment facility. This paper has clear merit in supporting others to deploy a similar set-up at their own institutions. It is particularly helpful that the paper describes the evolution of the lab rather than simply define its end state.

This work could have been strengthened by student feedback, however it is a valuable contribution nonetheless.

One other missing part that would have been helpful but not critical is lessons learned from the faculty perspective.  I.e. the cost of moving exams from a 1-shot paper mode to a multi-shot computer-based mode.
